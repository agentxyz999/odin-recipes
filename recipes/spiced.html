Spiced Corn Recipe

Description

A spicy and tangy chaat corn side dish for African pilli pilli chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh corn kernels
  • salt as needed
  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • ½ teaspoon chaat masala
  • ¼ teaspoon chili powder

Steps

  1. Fill a deep pan with water and bring to a boil. Add corn and salt and cook until corn is tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add boiled corn, lemon juice, garam masala, chaat masala, chili powder, and salt and cook until corn turns light brown in color, about 5 minutes
